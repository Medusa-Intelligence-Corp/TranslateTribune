Privacy Policy for TranslateTribune.com

Welcome to TranslateTribune.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit TranslateTribune.com and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

1. No Cookies

TranslateTribune.com does not use cookies for tracking purposes or for any other reason. Our website is designed to respect your privacy by avoiding the use of cookies entirely.

2. Minimal JavaScript Usage

TranslateTribune.com aims employs minimal JavaScript for enhancing the site's usability, specifically for animating the site's title and providing accessibility features for screen readers. This minimal use aligns with our commitment to avoid the "JavaScript Trap" as discussed in the Free Software Foundation's article. Our core JavaScript code does not meet any of the conditions that would classify it as nontrivial, thereby respecting user freedom and privacy. Note that our logic_puzzles.html page uses more advanced Javascript as is required for developing browser-based games.

3. No Trackers

We do not employ any form of tracking mechanisms, such as web beacons, pixels, or analytics scripts, to collect information about your activities on our site.

4. Server Logs Information

The only information we indirectly collect is through essential server logs maintained by AWS and Cloudfront. These logs record basic details such as your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, and timestamps, which are necessary for operational integrity, troubleshooting, and enhancing site security and performance. This process does not involve actively collecting or profiling personal information.

5. Data Protection

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you access TranslateTribune.com. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

TranslateTribune.com may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by email at editor@translatetribune.com.

By using our site, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.
